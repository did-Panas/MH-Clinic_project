How Medical Evaluations Determine the Right Medication for Erectile Dysfunction

After clinical diagnosis, medical examinations, a complete study of the case, and a study of the medical history, our specialized team of doctors determines whether treatment with medications will contribute to strengthening your erection or not, and determines the most appropriate types for your condition.

Drug therapy

It is part of the global medical protocol for treating the problem of erectile dysfunction, and it is resorted to in cases that are in the early stages of the problem, and the condition has not worsened to a higher level that requires more advanced procedures. The most appropriate case is chosen based on the following:

  • 01

    Degree of erectile dysfunction

    Understanding the degree of erectile dysfunction is crucial in selecting the most effective treatment plan. Our approach involves a thorough assessment to determine the severity of your condition, allowing us to tailor treatments that best suit your specific needs.

  • 02

    Does the person suffer from any chronic diseases

    Identify any chronic diseases the individual may have to customise treatment plans effectively. This helps in addressing underlying conditions and ensuring a comprehensive approach to their overall health and well-being.

  • 03

    Cause of erectile dysfunction in the case

    Investigate the underlying causes of erectile dysfunction in each case to develop a targeted treatment plan. Understanding these causes helps tailor interventions and address specific health factors contributing to the condition.

  • 04

    Patient's age

    Consider the patient's age to tailor treatment options effectively, as age can influence the underlying causes and management strategies for various health conditions.

Types of medications that we can resort to:

  • 01

    Sildenafil, known as Viagra

    Sildenafil, commonly known as Viagra, is a medication used to treat erectile dysfunction by increasing blood flow to the penis, helping achieve and maintain an erection. It is often prescribed based on individual health needs and can provide effective relief for many men.

  • 02

    Vardenafil

    Vardenafil is a medication used to treat erectile dysfunction by enhancing blood flow to the penis, facilitating the ability to achieve and sustain an erection. It works similarly to other erectile dysfunction treatments, providing effective relief based on individual health needs.

  • 03

    Cause of erectile dysfunction in the case

    Identify the specific causes of erectile dysfunction in each case to create a targeted treatment plan. This may involve evaluating factors such as medical conditions, lifestyle habits, and psychological influences to address the root issues effectively.

  • 04

    Tadafil

    Tadalafil, commonly known as Cialis, is a medication used to treat erectile dysfunction by increasing blood flow to the penis. It helps achieve and maintain an erection and can be effective for up to 36 hours, providing a longer window of opportunity compared to other treatments.

The effect of some of these medications may last for more than two days, while the effect of others wears off within one day.
